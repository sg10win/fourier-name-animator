Vector Fox

Vector Fox is a Fourier series visualizer that transforms any name or word into a living animation — a chain of spinning circles whose combined tip traces out the letters of your text.

It's part of a creative series exploring math, motion, and sound. Each name becomes a unique visual signature driven by the Discrete Fourier Transform.

How to use
01  Type your name in the input below (max 12 characters)
02  Press ANIMATE or hit Enter
03  Watch the epicycles trace your name
04  Adjust speed, circles & color to taste
05  Download as a GIF to share
